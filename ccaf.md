## AI Fluency Framework

### What is the AI Fluency Framework?
A framework for effectively working with AI systems by applying the 4Ds.

### 4Ds of AI Fluency

#### 1. Delegation
**Definition:** Determining what tasks should be assigned to AI.

**Key Concepts**
- Identify repetitive tasks
- Delegate research and summarization
- Use AI for drafting and brainstorming

**Example**
- Generate meeting summaries
- Create first drafts of documentation

---

#### 2. Description
**Definition:** Clearly communicating requirements to AI.

**Key Concepts**
- Provide context
- Specify output format
- Define constraints

**Example Prompt**
"Summarize this document in 5 bullet points for a technical audience."

---

#### 3. Discrimination
**Definition:** Evaluating AI-generated output critically.

**Key Concepts**
- Verify accuracy
- Check for hallucinations
- Validate sources

**Questions**
- Is the information accurate?
- Does the response satisfy requirements?
- Are there any missing details?

---

#### 4. Diligence
**Definition:** Monitoring and improving AI outputs over time.

**Key Concepts**
- Iterate prompts
- Review outputs
- Improve workflows

**Best Practices**
- Test multiple prompts
- Compare results
- Maintain quality standards

---

## Exam Notes

### Important Questions

#### Q: Which AI Fluency principle focuses on evaluating AI responses?
**Answer:** Discrimination

#### Q: Which principle focuses on assigning tasks to AI?
**Answer:** Delegation

#### Q: Which principle focuses on writing effective prompts?
**Answer:** Description

#### Q: Which principle focuses on continuous review and improvement?
**Answer:** Diligence
